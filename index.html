Questions & Answers

Everything You
Need to Know

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment. It involves the strategic planning and arrangement of furniture, colour schemes, lighting, and other elements to create functional and beautiful spaces that reflect the people who inhabit them.

Interior design improves the functionality, safety, and aesthetics of a space. A well-designed interior enhances comfort and efficiency, reflects personal or brand identity, and increases property value. Professional interior design ensures that every element works harmoniously to meet your needs — saving you time, money, and costly mistakes.

Chitron Interior offers a comprehensive range of services including residential and commercial interior design, space planning, project management, custom furniture design, colour consultation, 3D visualisation, and full renovation oversight. We work closely with clients to deliver tailored solutions that enhance the beauty and functionality of their spaces.

Timelines vary by scope. A single-room residential project typically takes 6–10 weeks from concept to completion. Full-home transformations range from 3–6 months. We provide a detailed schedule at the proposal stage and keep you updated at every milestone throughout the process.
